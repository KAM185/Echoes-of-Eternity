# Echoes of Eternity

**When ancient stones finally speak.**

Echoes of Eternity is an emotional AI cultural heritage experience powered by **Gemini 3 multimodal models**. Upload a monument photo and hear it speak — analyzing its history, architecture, preservation state, and telling its story in its own timeless voice.

## Features
- Gemini 3 vision + reasoning analysis
- Structured heritage assessment with damage overlays
- First-person emotional storytelling narration
- Audio playback (TTS + browser fallback)
- Conversational “Ask the Echo” chat with the monument
- Mobile-friendly, accessible, dark-mode design
